Miso Soup

A simple yet healthy traditional Japanese Soup

Original Recipe by allrecipes.

Ingredients:

  • 4 Cups Water
  • 2 Teaspoons Dashi Granules
  • 3 Tablespoons Miso Paste
  • 1 8-ounce Package Silken Tofu (Diced)
  • 2 Green Onions (1cm/0.5in Diagonal Slices)

Procedure:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine Water and Dashi Granules in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat then bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat to medium and whisk in Miso Paste.
  4. Stir in Tofu.
  5. Separate the layers of green onions, and add them to the soup. Simmer gently for 2-3 minutes, then serve.
