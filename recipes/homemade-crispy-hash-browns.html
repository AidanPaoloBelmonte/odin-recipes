Homemade Crispy Hashbrowns

A classic yet filling breakfast combo

Original Recipe by allrecipes.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Medium Russet Potatoes (Shredded)
  • 1/2 Medium Onion (Finely Chopped)
  • 1/2 Cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1 Cup Oil (For frying, or as needed)
  • Salt and Pepper (To taste)

Procedure:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Rinse shredded potatoes until water is clear, then drain and squeeze dry. Place shreds in a bowl and mix in the onion, flour and egg until evenly distributed.
  3. Heat around 1/4 inch oil in a large heavy skillet over medium-high heat. When the oil is sizzling hot, place the potatoes into the pan in a 1/2 thick layer, either covering the entire pan, or in small piles (similar to pancakes).
  4. Cook until nicely browned on the bottom, then flip over and brown on the other side, each side taking around 5 minutes to brown.
  5. Remove from the pan and drain on paper towels. Season with Salt and Pepper then serve immediately. Serve hot and enjoy!
