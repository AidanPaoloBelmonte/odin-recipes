Filipino-Style Congee

A simple yet popular Rice Porridge dish from the Philippines

Original Recipe by allrecipes.

Ingredients:

  • 5 Cups Water
  • 2 Cups Uncooked Rice
  • 3 Tablespoons Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Whole Head Garlic (Minced)
  • 1 Tablespoons Sliced Ginger
  • 2 Tablespoons Sliced Onion
  • 2 Skinless, Boneless, Chicken Breasts (Cubed)
  • 1 Tablespoon Fish Sauce (To taste)
  • Ground Black Pepper (To taste)
  • 1 Teaspoon Annatto Powder
  • 2 Hard-Boiled Eggs (Peeled, or to taste)
  • 2 Tablespoons Chopped Spring Onions (to taste)
  1. Bring water and rice to a boil, stirring constantly until rice is soft (~25 minutes)
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Cook and stir garlic in the hot oil until golden brown (~5 minutes). Transfer half of the garlic to a small bowl.
  3. Stir ginger into garlic remaining in the saucepan; cook and stir until fragrant (1 minute).
  4. Add onion then cook and stir until soft (~5 minutes).
  5. Add chicken, then season with fish sauce and pepper. Cook and Stir until chicken is no longer pink in the center (~5 minutes).
  6. Stir chicken mixture into the rice. Dust Annatto powder over rice using a strainer and stir until dissolved.
  7. Add hard-boiled eggs. Simmer on low, stirring constantly, until the rice reaches a porridge-like consistency (~5 minutes). Remove from heat and stir in spring onions.
  8. Garnish bowl of congee with reserved fried garlic.
